Contact Us

We’re here to assist with any questions, inquiries, or feedback. Feel free to connect with us through the following channels:

Email Us:​

  • For general inquiries, reach out to us at ciroh-it-admin@ua.edu for any general questions or assistance.
  • If you’d like to add content or make changes to this site, please contact us at ciroh-it-admin@ua.edu.

Connect with Us:​

  • Join our discussions on Slack by connecting to the ciroh-ua-it-support channel.
  • Join our Monthly Office Hours to engage with experts and stay updated on the latest developments in CIROH Research Cyberinfrastructure and Community NextGen.

Social Media:​

Stay connected with CIROH on our social media platforms for the latest updates, news, and insights into our ongoing projects.
